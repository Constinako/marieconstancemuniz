Who I am

I've spent most of my career helping organizations navigate complexity. Whether building products, redesigning operations, or introducing new systems — I'm most useful when growth, competing priorities, and ambiguity make execution difficult.

My role is often to understand what's happening, create clarity, and help teams move forward: shaping a product, redesigning a process, aligning stakeholders, or building systems from scratch. The tools change. The underlying challenge is usually the same.

What I'm Good At

Core strengths

Cross-Functional Leadership

Aligning stakeholders across engineering, product, commercial, and ops — especially when priorities don't naturally converge.

Process & Systems Design

Finding where things get stuck, redesigning the flow, and building systems that are repeatable without depending on heroics.

Product Strategy & Prioritization

Deciding what to build, what not to build, and why — from roadmap ownership to cutting what isn't earning its place.

Customer Discovery & Prototyping

Data & Insights
